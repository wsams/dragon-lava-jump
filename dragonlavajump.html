How to play

Controls: ← → Move  |  ↓ = Slam down (in air)  |  Space = Jump (double jump in air)  |  F = Boost (once per jump, in air)  |  G = Fire breath  •   Or use the onscreen controls.

Goal: Reach the golden pillar. Collect all 30 dots. Avoid lava (death). Cave: Don't touch slimes, bats, crawlers, or stalactites (reset). Desert: Don't touch cacti, scorpions, buzzards, or flying needles (reset). Touch poles to set checkpoints; collect the orange orb for a short lava bounce; touch the fire totem for flame (G) until you're hit. Some platforms (Cave) drop after you stand on them.
